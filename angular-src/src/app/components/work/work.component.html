ExpenseManager (Mobile only)
arrow_back_ios arrow_forward_ios
Just another Expense Manager? Sure, but this one is designed to consist of only the core features that are required to track ones daily expenses. There are tons of apps already out there, but I never find the one that is exactly the way I want it to be, so I end up doing it myself. The peace in mind that the data is secure and no update will mess the App up is just great!

It also considers people with small screens such as the iPhone 5/SE since a lot of Apps have such a large header & footer that there is barely any space left for the content.

Furthermore optionally, an expense can be assigned to a group like "Iceland trip" which will be listed in the "groups" tab. So after all your travelling over the years you can scroll through the list of trips/groups and see exactly how much you paid, and set a filter to only show those expenses on the overview/home screen.

It is a PWA made with Angular 9 and operates fully locally through IndexedDB. A Demo can be found here and the Repository can be found here. Since this is a mobile only design, when viewing the Demo, please open the dev-tools and resize the page to for example the iPhone 5 preset if you use Chrome.
TravelStats (Mobile only)

This App lets you keep track of all the trips and travelling you do. You can add countries, flights and trips to see exactly where you were when and how much you spend. The dashboard then shows you how many countries you have visited, how many flights you had, how many days of your life you have spend travelling any many other stats.

It is a PWA made with Angular 9 and operates fully locally through IndexedDB. A Demo can be found here and the Repository can be found here. Since this is a mobile only design, when viewing the Demo, please open the dev-tools and resize the page to for example the iPhone 5 preset if you use Chrome.
